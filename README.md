# ReBuildHub

## 🌍 Overview
Construction and demolition generate 30–35% of EU solid waste. Most reusable materials are discarded not because they lack value, but because no structured decision process exists on site.

ReBuildHub is an open, lightweight digital infrastructure that enables real-time material decisions directly at the point of action.

In under 30 seconds, a site operative can log whether a material is:
- Retained  
- Transferred  
- Salvaged  
- Discarded  

Each decision is stored as an immutable, append-only event, producing audit-ready data compatible with the EU Digital Product Passport framework.

## 🧠 Key Features
- ⚡ 30-second decision workflow (no training required)  
- 📱 Mobile-first Progressive Web App (no installation)  
- 🔌 Offline-first architecture (works without internet)  
- 🔐 Immutable, append-only event logging  
- 🌍 Built on open standards (schema.org, W3C Linked Data)  
- 🏗️ Designed for real construction site conditions  

---

## 🏗️ Why It Matters
Existing solutions in the construction and circular economy space operate after materials are removed or discarded.

ReBuildHub intervenes at the moment of decision on site, where material fate is determined.

This approach enables:
- Real-time, structured data generation at source  
- Improved material reuse  
- Measurable waste reduction  
- Audit-ready ESG and compliance data
